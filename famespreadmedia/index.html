Founder's Story

Back when I was 18, fresh outta high school, Covid came outta nowhere and turned everything upside down. It was a wild ride!

I got myself admitted into an engineering college and being a tech enthusiast, I started diving deep into computers and mobile phones. And in 2022, I started content curation and creation and it was a huge hit! I gained a whopping 5k followers in just 2 months, with a reach of over 10 million on my profile!

But I didn't stop there. I started giving advice to people on how to start their own social media journey and grow their following by brainstorming ideas for them. And to level up even more, I decided to dig into Digital Marketing and got myself certified. (We gotta keep expanding those horizons)

That's when I took a leap of faith and started my career as a freelancer in Digital Marketing. I hustled day and night, never giving up on my dream to open my own Digital Marketing company. And guess what?

In 2023, I achieved my first major milestone, landing a full-time project. That's when I knew it was time to take it up a notch, I started expanding and launched my very own company “FAME SPREAD MEDIA”. The name says it all, right? We are all about helping brands gain fame and spread their work. It's been an incredible journey!

And now, at the young age of 22, I can proudly say that I'm living the life of an entrepreneur, following my passion. Sure, there have been failures along the way, I even felt like giving up because
managing college and building my company was not an easy task but you know what they say, it's the failures that make us stronger and keep us going. So, if you can dream it, you can definitely
achieve it! Keep chasing those dreams and never give up!
